# 6-Week Cutting Workout Schedule
**Profile:** 28-year-old Male, 160 lbs  
**Goal:** Fat loss while preserving muscle mass  
**Duration:** 6 weeks (can be extended)

## Training Split: Push/Pull/Legs + Cardio (6 days/week)

### **Week 1-2: Foundation Phase**

#### **Day 1: Push (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)**
1. **Barbell Bench Press** - 4 sets x 8-10 reps
2. **Incline Dumbbell Press** - 3 sets x 10-12 reps
3. **Overhead Press** - 4 sets x 8-10 reps
4. **Lateral Raises** - 3 sets x 12-15 reps
5. **Dips** - 3 sets x 10-12 reps
6. **Close-Grip Bench Press** - 3 sets x 10-12 reps
7. **Tricep Rope Pushdowns** - 3 sets x 12-15 reps

**Finisher:** 10 minutes HIIT cardio (30 sec on, 30 sec off)

#### **Day 2: Pull (Back, Biceps)**
1. **Deadlifts** - 4 sets x 6-8 reps
2. **Pull-ups/Chin-ups** - 4 sets x 6-10 reps
3. **Barbell Rows** - 4 sets x 8-10 reps
4. **T-Bar Rows** - 3 sets x 10-12 reps
5. **Face Pulls** - 3 sets x 15-20 reps
6. **Barbell Curls** - 3 sets x 10-12 reps
7. **Hammer Curls** - 3 sets x 12-15 reps

**Finisher:** 15 minutes steady-state cardio

#### **Day 3: Legs (Quads, Hamstrings, Glutes, Calves)**
1. **Back Squats** - 4 sets x 8-10 reps
2. **Romanian Deadlifts** - 4 sets x 10-12 reps
3. **Bulgarian Split Squats** - 3 sets x 12 each leg
4. **Leg Press** - 3 sets x 15-20 reps
5. **Walking Lunges** - 3 sets x 12 each leg
6. **Calf Raises** - 4 sets x 15-20 reps
7. **Leg Curls** - 3 sets x 12-15 reps

**Finisher:** 10 minutes HIIT cardio

#### **Day 4: Push (Volume Focus)**
1. **Incline Barbell Press** - 4 sets x 10-12 reps
2. **Dumbbell Flyes** - 3 sets x 12-15 reps
3. **Dumbbell Shoulder Press** - 4 sets x 10-12 reps
4. **Arnold Press** - 3 sets x 12-15 reps
5. **Cable Lateral Raises** - 3 sets x 15-20 reps
6. **Overhead Tricep Extension** - 3 sets x 12-15 reps
7. **Diamond Push-ups** - 3 sets x max reps

**Finisher:** 20 minutes steady-state cardio

#### **Day 5: Pull (Volume Focus)**
1. **Cable Rows** - 4 sets x 10-12 reps
2. **Lat Pulldowns** - 4 sets x 10-12 reps
3. **Single-Arm Dumbbell Rows** - 3 sets x 12 each arm
4. **Reverse Flyes** - 3 sets x 15-20 reps
5. **Shrugs** - 3 sets x 12-15 reps
6. **Cable Curls** - 3 sets x 12-15 reps
7. **21s (Bicep Curls)** - 2 sets

**Finisher:** 15 minutes HIIT cardio

#### **Day 6: Legs & Core**
1. **Front Squats** - 4 sets x 10-12 reps
2. **Stiff Leg Deadlifts** - 4 sets x 12-15 reps
3. **Goblet Squats** - 3 sets x 15-20 reps
4. **Hip Thrusts** - 3 sets x 15-20 reps
5. **Plank** - 3 sets x 60 seconds
6. **Russian Twists** - 3 sets x 20 each side
7. **Mountain Climbers** - 3 sets x 30 seconds

**Finisher:** 25 minutes steady-state cardio

#### **Day 7: Active Recovery**
- Light walking (30-45 minutes)
- Stretching/yoga (20 minutes)
- Foam rolling

---

### **Week 3-4: Intensity Phase**

*Increase weights by 5-10% and add one extra set to compound movements*

**Changes:**
- Compound exercises: 5 sets instead of 4
- Reduce rest time by 15 seconds
- Increase cardio intensity

---

### **Week 5-6: Peak Phase**

*Focus on metabolic stress and higher volume*

**Changes:**
- Add drop sets to isolation exercises
- Increase cardio to 6 days/week
- Add morning fasted cardio (20 minutes, 3x/week)

---

## Nutrition Guidelines for Cutting

### **Daily Macros (approximately):**
- **Calories:** 1,800-2,000 (adjust based on progress)
- **Protein:** 160-180g (1g-1.1g per lb bodyweight)
- **Carbs:** 120-150g (focus around workouts)
- **Fats:** 60-80g

### **Meal Timing:**
- **Pre-workout:** Banana + coffee (30-45 min before)
- **Post-workout:** Protein shake + simple carbs (within 30 min)
- **Regular meals:** Every 3-4 hours

### **Food Choices:**
**Proteins:** Chicken breast, lean beef, fish, eggs, protein powder  
**Carbs:** Rice, oats, sweet potato, fruits, vegetables  
**Fats:** Nuts, olive oil, avocado, fish oil  

### **Hydration:**
- Minimum 1 gallon (3.8L) water daily
- Extra 16-20oz during workouts

---

## Cardio Schedule

### **HIIT Protocols:**
- **Bike/Treadmill:** 30 seconds all-out, 30 seconds easy recovery
- **Rowing:** 20 seconds max effort, 40 seconds easy
- **Burpees:** 45 seconds work, 15 seconds rest

### **Steady-State Options:**
- Incline walking (3.5+ mph, 5-8% incline)
- Cycling (moderate pace)
- Swimming
- Elliptical

---

## Progression Tracking

### **Weekly Measurements:**
- **Weight:** Same time daily (morning, after bathroom)
- **Body Fat:** Body fat scale or calipers
- **Measurements:** Waist, chest, arms, thighs
- **Photos:** Front, side, back (same lighting/time)

### **Performance Metrics:**
- Track weights and reps for all exercises
- Monitor energy levels (1-10 scale)
- Sleep quality (hours + quality rating)

### **Adjustment Guidelines:**
- **Weight loss stalled:** Reduce calories by 100-150/day
- **Losing too fast:** Increase calories by 100-150/day
- **Low energy:** Add refeed day (increase carbs by 100-150g)

### **Fat Loss Support:**
- **Green Tea Extract:** With meals
- **CLA:** 3-6g daily with meals

## Important Notes

1. **Rest:** 7-9 hours sleep nightly for optimal recovery
2. **Stress Management:** Practice meditation or stress-reduction techniques
3. **Consistency:** Adherence is more important than perfection
4. **Listen to Your Body:** Take extra rest days if overly fatigued
5. **Progress Photos:** Take weekly progress photos for motivation

### **When to Adjust:**
- No weight loss for 7-10 days: Reduce calories or increase cardio
- Losing more than 2 lbs/week: Increase calories slightly
- Strength declining rapidly: Add refeed day or diet break

---

**Expected Results:** 1-2 lbs fat loss per week while maintaining muscle mass. Total expected loss: 6-12 lbs over 6 weeks.

**Next Phase:** After 6 weeks, consider a 1-2 week diet break or transition to lean bulking phase depending on goals.
